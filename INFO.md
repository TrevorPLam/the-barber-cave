## Executive Summary

The Barber Cave is a luxury barbershop located in Dallas, Texas, offering premium grooming services in an upscale urban environment. **As of 2026, the brand has expanded internationally with a second location in London, UK**, positioning it as a global grooming destination. Owned and operated by **Trevalyn M. Parker** (professionally known as Trill LadiBarber), a female barber and SMP (Scalp Micropigmentation) specialist, the shop maintains a strong reputation with **4.9 stars on Squire (194+ reviews)** and **4.7 stars on Birdeye (121+ reviews)**. 

Despite their premium positioning, loyal customer base, and international expansion, they currently operate without an owned website, relying entirely on third-party booking platforms (Squire, Booksy) and social media for their digital presence—a critical vulnerability for a brand with global ambitions.
